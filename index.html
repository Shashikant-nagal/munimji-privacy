Privacy Policy - MunimJi

MunimJi is an expense management app that helps users manage their income and track daily expenses.

1. Information We Collect

To enable account creation and login functionality, MunimJi collects the following information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)

This data is collected during user registration using secure authentication services.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used only for:

  • User authentication (login and signup)
  • Account management

We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

  • Account-related data (such as name and email) is securely stored using Firebase Authentication.
  • Expense-related data is stored locally on your device using MMKV and SQLite.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. All data transmitted is encrypted using secure protocols (HTTPS).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties.
