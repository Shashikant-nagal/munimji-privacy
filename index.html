Privacy Policy - MunimJi

Effective Date: 28 June 2026

MunimJi is an expense management application that helps users manage their income, track daily expenses, and securely back up and synchronize their data across devices. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

To provide the features of MunimJi, we may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile information provided by the user
  • Expense and financial records created within the app

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to provide and improve the services offered by MunimJi.

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Authenticate users securely using Firebase Authentication.
  • Back up and synchronize your data across your devices using Firebase Firestore.
  • Provide app functionality and improve the user experience.
  • Manage app configuration, including force update notifications, using Firebase Remote Config.
  • Display advertisements using Google AdMob.

3. Data Storage

  • Account information (such as name, email address, and phone number) is managed through Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile information and synchronized application data are securely stored in Google Firebase Firestore.
  • Your expense data is stored locally on your device using MMKV and SQLite. If backup and synchronization are enabled, relevant data is also securely synchronized with Firebase Firestore.
  • Firebase Remote Config is used to manage certain application settings, such as notifying users about mandatory app updates.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Communication between the app and our services is encrypted using secure HTTPS connections. While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

5. Third-Party Services

MunimJi uses trusted third-party services to provide certain features. These services have their own privacy policies and may process information necessary to provide their functionality.

  • Google Firebase Authentication
  • Google Firebase Firestore
  • Google Firebase Remote Config
  • Google AdMob

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Advertising

MunimJi uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect and process certain information, including but not limited to:

  • Advertising ID (where available)
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • App interaction information

This information may be used to provide, measure, improve, and protect advertising services.
