MunimJi - Account Deletion Request

At MunimJi, we respect your privacy and give you control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to info@corevantaorbit.com.
  2. Use the subject line: "Delete my MunimJi account".
  3. Include the email address associated with your MunimJi account.

We will verify your request and process the deletion within 3–5 working days.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your MunimJi account.
  • Profile information.
  • Expense and financial records stored in the cloud.
  • Backup and synchronization data associated with your account.

Data That May Be Retained

We do not intentionally retain your personal data after your deletion request has been processed, unless retention is required by applicable law or to resolve legal disputes.
